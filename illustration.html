musubu logo

musubu is my on-going project with two other developers.

"musubu" means "to connect" or "to tie a knot".
The logo is not final yet and these are six ideas to choose from. I tried to incorporate the meaning of musubu by either having a knot illustration as a part of the logo or modify middle "s" and "u" to connect "mu" and "bu"

I got the inspiration for the style of knots from how Japanese people knot a fortune-telling paper (omikuji) to a branch of a tree in the shrine.

Bakery grand opening poster

I made this on adobe photoshop.
I myself is a home baker, follows other home bakers on Instagram. I saw one home baker was commenting that she needs a poster for her newly opening bakery business. I took this opportunity to make this poster to offer her help.

I chose Amatic SC and Wawati SC for the bakery name font. These lightweight, soft fonts remind warm and soft freshly baked bread. I used 2 colors that mutually enhance each other but also they are great background colors for the brown color of croissant image..

The name of the business has been changed because she ends up not using this poster. She only wanted a white and black simple poster without any picture.

Even though the poster wasn't used, this was a great opportunity for me to practice design skills, and to take the first step to reach out to people might need help.

E-commerce shop : UI design

This was a one of Daily UI challenge I did.
Theme was given by Daily UI every week. This time challenge was to design UI for single item page for E-commerce shop. I decided to design a page for a cosmetic brand.

First I wrote down ideas for this shop such as name, item, brand theme and target user. Next I picked about 12 fonts that matches brand theme and picked 2 fonts to use from there.

I designed logo for this brand on Adobe illustrator then, I rendered 3D image of item on Adobe Dimention. Finally, I designed both mobile and desktop single item page on Adobe XD.
