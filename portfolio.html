My first attempt at making a portfolio

I added this page because I approached building my portfolio website as I would any project. This website you are currently viewing is my second iteration. Below I will highlight my design decisions.

old

This was the homepage. There was only a header and hero image. Nothing more. The image, which I continued to use, has every letter of my last name. The overall website interface is simple so this image will add color to pop. On the second iteration of the website, I added a little bit about myself and images with links to guide to each page under the hero image.

new

Here is the change I made with the mobile view. I had a different interface for the mobile view before, but I tried to keep the same interface of the desktop view as much as possible. I changed my mind about the hamburger icon for the menu because there were only three things on it. To keep it simple, I adjusted the position of the menu bar.

I enjoy the process of product designing and I am hoping to do more. I am a dedicated self-starter, passionate about making people happy.
