Hello, my name is Sayaka Tadachi.

I'm a UI/UX designer.

Thank you for visiting my website. I made this website as my portfolio. It includes my work as a product designer and photographer. Also, little bonus at the end, my favorite brownie recipe because I'm sure we can all appreciate good brownies.


When I started building the website, my knowledge of coding was close to zero. I learned basic HTML and CSS online and kept learning as I was building my first website. I managed to code a website, but immediately I thought, I can do better. What you have on your screen right now is my second attempt. I have made a website before using a hosting service, but I wanted to challenge myself and built it from scratch. By coding the website structure myself, I have a better understanding of product development, which can be reflected in my design thinking.


I strive to learn more to be a better designer. My goal is to increase my knowledge and skills in inclusive design and in creating accessible experiences. I believe that good design is design that in useable by anyone.
