blanc blanc

Overview

This project was to design an e-commerce website for the clothing brand "blanc blanc".

blanc blanc is a newly launched brand, based in Toronto. This brand is proud to offer products that are ethically-made, high-quality and affordable prices.


The client wants this website to be clean and approachable like their brand theme. Also they think the website should creat a more shopping in the store feeling rather than looking through a catalog.

Personas

Erika

35 / Manager / Toronto

Erika is a manager at international cosmetic company. She earns a salary of $74,000 per year. She lives with 2 kids and husband in downtown Toronto.

Having kids made her more environmentally conscious but that doesn’t mean she want to compromise style and price for fashion. She is looking for clothing that is stylish, affordable and comfortable enough to be able to play with her kids.

Josh

29 / Shop Owner / Toronto

Josh owns a successful coffee shop in Vancouver. He earns an average of $4,500 a month. He is single and spends most of his time at his coffee shop.

He has to be stylish and presentable while at work at coffee shop but he wants to provide his customers a place they can relax so he usually wears casual clothing such as T-shirts and jeans to set a tone. He is always looking for unique clothing and never buys them from chain fashion brands.

He is also a huge supporter for local business since he is one of them. He uses local contractors, artists and suppliers for his business.

Cortney

20 / University Student / Guelph

Cortney is a student at Guelph University. She is studying to be a veterinarian. She loves animals and cares about the environment. She lives with 3 other friends in a student house. Though she has a part-time job at a local restaurant, she can only work on weekends so she doesn’t have a lot of money to spend on clothing.

She has a 3 years old Golden retriever and they enjoy hiking together. Also, she’s been practicing yoga for almost 3 years and likes to wear comfortable and sporty clothing.

Wireframes


Wireframes are carefully designed to have enough white-space and pictures of products.

The balanced and organized web-page provides an environment for users to be able to focus on one product at a time while still showing other products on the same page.
